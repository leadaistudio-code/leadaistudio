Before you book

Contact & demo questions

Is the demo really free?

Yes — completely. The 30-minute call costs nothing and carries no obligation. Our goal is to give you a clear, honest read on where AI can help, whether or not you decide to work with us.

How long is the call?

About 30 minutes. That's enough time to review your key workflows, surface the highest-impact opportunities and outline next steps. If your situation is more involved, we'll simply book a longer follow-up.

Do we need to prepare anything?

No prep is required. It helps to come with one or two workflows that frustrate your team or consume a lot of hours — but if you're still exploring, we can work from a blank page just as easily.

What happens after the demo?

You'll receive a short written summary of the opportunities we discussed. Where it makes sense, we propose a fixed-price 2-week pilot so you can prove measurable ROI before any larger commitment.
