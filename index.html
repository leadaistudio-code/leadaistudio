Questions

Frequently asked questions

What does Lead AI Studio actually do?

We design, secure and run production AI for corporate and small-business teams — workflow automation, RAG knowledge systems, LLM cost optimization, LLMOps and governance, private AI infrastructure, and AI agents for operations.

How do you keep our corporate data secure?

Every deployment uses zero-data-retention pipelines, AI guardrails, private VPC or AWS Bedrock infrastructure, encryption, role-based access and audit trails — aligned with SOC 2, GDPR and ISO 42001.

Can you really reduce our AI / LLM costs?

Yes. Through multi-model orchestration, token reduction, AI gateways and cost observability, our clients typically see 40–70% lower LLM spend — usually recovering the project cost within a quarter.

Do you work with small businesses, not just enterprises?

Absolutely. Our Launch plan is built for small offices and SOHO teams, while Growth and Enterprise plans serve scaling and corporate organizations.

How do we get started?

Book a free demo. We'll review your workflows, identify the highest-impact opportunity, and usually propose a fixed-price 2-week pilot so you can prove ROI before any larger commitment.
