Questions

Pricing FAQ

Can we start small and scale up?

Yes. Most clients begin on the Launch or Studio plan with one or two automation workflows, then move up as the value is proven. Plans share the same secure foundation, so upgrading to Scale or an enterprise package is a smooth, no-rebuild transition with no lost work.

What does the 2-week pilot include?

The fixed-price 2-week enterprise pilot scopes one high-impact use case, builds it in a secure environment, and delivers a working prototype plus an ROI report. It is a low-risk proof of concept with no long-term commitment, so leadership can evaluate real results before a larger build.

Are there setup or integration fees?

SOHO and small-business plans are flat monthly subscriptions with onboarding included — no separate setup fee for standard configurations. Complex integrations, private infrastructure or data migrations are scoped and quoted up front so there are no surprises.

Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?

Yes. The Platform and Governed Enterprise packages are priced to scope — based on the number of workflows and agents, infrastructure model, governance and compliance requirements, and support level. We provide a detailed fixed proposal after a short discovery call.
