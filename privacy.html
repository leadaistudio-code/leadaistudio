Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, protect and share information — and the choices and rights you have.

Last updated: 17 May 2026

1. Who we are

Lead AI Studio ("we", "us", "our") provides secure enterprise AI engineering and automation services. This policy explains how we handle personal information when you visit this website or engage our services. For any privacy question, contact contact@leadaistudio.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you provide — your name, work email, company, job title and any details you submit through our demo request, newsletter or contact forms.
  • Usage data — pages visited, referring links and approximate region, collected through privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics that do not track you across sites.
  • Engagement data — information shared with us during a project, handled under the separate agreement and security terms covering that engagement.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to demo requests, enquiries and proposals.
  • To deliver, support and improve our services.
  • To send insights or updates you have subscribed to — you can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To understand, secure and improve this website.
  • To meet legal, accounting and contractual obligations.

4. Legal bases

Where the GDPR applies, we process personal data on the basis of your consent, our legitimate interest in operating and improving our business, the performance of a contract, or compliance with a legal obligation.

5. Cookies & analytics

This site does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. We use a cookieless, privacy-respecting analytics tool to measure aggregate, anonymous usage. A short notice lets you accept or decline analytics; your choice is stored locally in your browser.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, hosting, scheduling and email), under contracts that require appropriate protection, or where required by law.

7. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. Engagement data is governed by zero-data-retention pipelines and the terms of the relevant project agreement.

8. Security

We apply encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access, audit trails and least-privilege practices. See our Security page for detail.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or port your personal data, to object to or restrict processing, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any right, email contact@leadaistudio.com and we will respond within the time required by applicable law.

10. International transfers

We operate with global clients and providers. Where personal data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

11. Children

Our website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by a revised "last updated" date above.

13. Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email contact@leadaistudio.com or reach us via our contact page.
