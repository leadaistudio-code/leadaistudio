Security at Lead AI Studio

Trust is engineered into every deployment from day one. Here is how we protect data.

Last updated: 17 May 2026

Our approach

Corporate clients share data with us only when they trust us with it. We treat security as a design requirement, not an afterthought — every engagement is built to keep sensitive data under your control.

Zero-data-retention

Our pipelines are designed so that your data is processed in isolation and not stored or used to train models. Where retention is required for a feature, it is explicit, scoped and agreed in the engagement contract.

AI guardrails

We apply input and output validation to prevent unsafe, off-policy or hallucinated responses, with policy controls tuned to each client's risk profile.

Private infrastructure

Deployments run in your own VPC or on AWS Bedrock, with model endpoints isolated to your environment. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Access control

We enforce role-based access, least-privilege permissions and multi-factor authentication for our team. Full audit trails record who accessed what and when.

Monitoring & observability

Production systems are continuously monitored for quality, drift, cost and anomalies, so issues are surfaced before they reach users.

Compliance alignment

Our architecture is aligned with the principles of SOC 2, GDPR and ISO 42001. We are happy to sign an NDA before any sensitive detail is shared and to complete vendor security reviews.

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in this website or our services, please report it privately to contact@leadaistudio.com. We ask that you give us reasonable time to investigate and remediate before any public disclosure, and we will not pursue good-faith research conducted under this policy.

Contact

For security questions, due-diligence documentation or to report an issue, email contact@leadaistudio.com.
